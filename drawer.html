Drawer

Drawer can be used to create sidebar navigation, change log and notification area as well.

Drawer Left

Click on the element below to open the drawer from left.

Drawer Left
Copy <div class="wsp-drawer">
    <button type="button" class="wsp-button button-primary" id="openLeftDrawer">Open Left Drawer</button>
    <div class="wsp-drawer-content wsp-drawer-left" id="leftDwawer">
        <div class="wsp-drawer-header">
            <h5>Drawer Left</h5>
            <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="wsp-close-btn" id="closeLeftDrawer">
                <i class="wsp-icon wsp-icon-x-small"></i>
            </a>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
